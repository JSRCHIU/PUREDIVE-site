COURSES

教學小班制

Small Group classes

純潛水提供全球認可的PADI與SDI水肺潛水證照課程，四人小班制確保你的安全與學習品質

We offer globally recognized PADI and SDI scuba certification courses. With a 4-student limit per class, we ensure your safety and learning quality.

地點堪比基隆副都心

Location

右轉美食、左轉潛點，純潛水位於基隆市中心與熱門潛點中間，交通便利。課程期間提供住宿，省去來回往返，更能專心學習與休息！

Pure Dive is between downtown and top diving spots. Accommodation included, so you can focus on diving and relaxing.
